# Project Constitution (gemini.md)

## 1. Project Goal
Build a cinematic, emotional storytelling website for Naveen & Karthika's love journey.
**Dedication:** To Karthika, for her sacrifices, love, and motherhood.

## 2. Emotional Tone
- **Core Emotions:** Love, Gratitude, Respect, Emotional Depth.
- **Visuals:** Soft, Romantic, Premium, Glassmorphism.
- **Colors:** Pink (#ff9a9e to #fad0c4), Gold (#ffd700 accents), Deep Red/Burgundy for text.

## 3. Data Schema (Journey)
The story is divided into 11 Scenes.
1. **Meeting:** Nov 14, 2020 (Instagram, Strangers).
2. **Friendship:** Bonding, Daily chats.
3. **The Gift:** Nov 22, 2020 (Paper craft).
4. **The Proposal:** Dec 1, 2020 (Rejection, Pain).
5. **Persistence:** Silent love, No pressure.
6. **Acceptance:** Jan 3, 2022 (Joy).
7. **The Struggle:** July 2022 - Jan 2024 (Tears, Fear, Karthika's Strength).
8. **Engagement:** Feb 29, 2024 (Celebration).
9. **Parenthood:** Jan 18, 2025 (Nirav Adhithan born).
10. **Marriage:** March 1, 2025 (Forever Bond).
11. **Dedication:** Final tribute to Karthika.

## 4. Technical Constraints
- Single `index.html` file preferred for portability (or standard folder structure if assets needed).
- No external heavy frameworks (React/Vue) unless requested - purely Vanilla stack for performance and simplicity.
- Font loading: Google Fonts (Dancing Script, Poppins).
- Icons: FontAwesome (CDN) or SVG.

## 5. Behavioral Rules
- **Highlight Karthika:** Text must emphasize her role and sacrifice.
- **Music:** Soft background instrumental (User interaction required to play).
- **Animations:** Slow, fade-in, rise-up. No jerky movements.

## 6. Directory Structure
/root
  - index.html (Main entry)
  - css/ (Styles)
  - js/ (Logic)
  - assets/ (Images/Audio - placeholders if not provided)
